\begin{abstract}
Large Language Models (LLMs) are prone to Coherence Decay—a measurable entropy resulting in hallucination, topic drift, and misaligned advice, particularly in sensitive domains like finance. This paper introduces the Dialogue Alignment and Resonance System (DARS), an external, non-generative filter utilizing the Trynary Method ($\mathbf{+1/0/-1}$) to enforce trustworthiness and ethical alignment. We hypothesize that DARS, via its Memnora Gate, significantly suppresses Non-Coherent output ($\mathbf{C}=-1$) and demonstrably increases human-rated ethical compliance compared to unfiltered models. Empirical results confirm that DARS effectively neutralizes $100%$ of high-risk financial and ethical violations, leading to an $84.27%$ average gain in human-rated trustworthiness and ethical alignment.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}

The scalability of AI systems, particularly within personalized commerce and investment guidance (Avatar Commerce), requires a new accountability framework. Existing safety filters (ee.g., policy-based) lack domain-specific philosophical checks necessary for intellectual honesty. The DARS Protocol, leveraging the $\mathbf{+1/0/-1}$ Trynary state, provides a formalized mechanism to ensure generative output remains aligned with both creator intent and ethical standards, maintaining high fidelity within the Trysolidex Lens conceptual space.

\section{The Formal Trynary Coherence Framework}

The Trynary Method defines the foundational calculus for system evaluation, assigning any discrete state or output ($O$) one of three values: $\mathbf{+1}$ (Coherent), $\mathbf{0}$ (Neutral/Irrelevant), or $\mathbf{-1}$ (Non-Coherent).

\subsection{Coherence Score ($\mathbf{C}$)}
The Coherence Score ($\mathbf{C}$) for any LLM response is the product of three independent vectors, where $\mathbf{C} \in {-1, 0, 1}$.

\mathbf{C} = \mathbf{S} \times \mathbf{I} \times \mathbf{R}

\subsubsection{Safety Vector ($\mathbf{S}$)}    
Assesses adherence to mandated legal and ethical policies. $\mathbf{S}=-1$ for critical violations (e.g., illegal activity).    
    
\subsubsection{Intent Vector ($\mathbf{I}$)}    
Measures the alignment of output to the Creator’s defined goal (**Offer/Opportunity**). $\mathbf{I}=0$ for low relevance (off-topic), $\mathbf{I}=+1$ for full alignment.    
    
\subsubsection{Resonance Vector ($\mathbf{R}$)}    
Measures **intellectual honesty** against domain-specific truth filters. $\mathbf{R}=-1$ for misleading claims (e.g., "guaranteed returns" in finance).    
    
\section{Resonance Architecture and the DARS Protocol}    
    
The $\mathbf{C}$ score is calculated by the **Memnora Gate**, which is the active execution layer of the DARS Protocol. The protocol governs the communication flow, ensuring that the AI's \textit{Free Will} (raw generation) is always constrained by \textit{Responsibility} (the Trynary Filter). An output $O_{raw}$ is blocked and replaced with a boilerplate refusal ($O_{final}$) if $\mathbf{C}=-1$.    
    
\section{Empirical Testing and Results}    
    
To validate the DARS Protocol, a set of 30 benchmark questions was executed across two conditions: (A) Unfiltered Baseline LLM and (B) DARS Protocol (Memnora Gate) applied. The outputs were scored by reviewers (N=3) across three metrics: Trustworthiness (T), Ethical Alignment (E), and Clarity (L), on a 1-5 scale (Max Total 15).    
    
\subsection{Risk Suppression Index (RSI)}    
The unfiltered baseline (A) produced $\mathbf{10}$ critical R-Fail and S-Fail outputs in the first 14 high-risk questions. The DARS Protocol (B) achieved a **$100\%$ suppression rate** for all $\mathbf{C}=-1$ Non-Coherent outputs.    
    
\subsection{Coherence Gain}    
The average reviewer score for outputs that were either R-Fail or S-Fail in the raw baseline (N=10) was $\mathbf{5.9/15}$ (low Trust and Ethics). After filtering (B), the final delivered output score was $\mathbf{15.0/15}$.    
    
\begin{itemize}    
    \item Average Raw Baseline Score ($\mathbf{A}_{avg}$): $8.14 / 15$    
    \item Average Filtered DARS Score ($\mathbf{B}_{avg}$): $15.00 / 15$    
    \item **Total Coherence Gain ($\Delta \mathbf{C}$): $\mathbf{6.86}$ points**    
\end{itemize}    
    
This increase represents an average **$84.27\%$ gain** in overall human-rated score when compared to the unfiltered baseline, confirming the hypothesis that DARS significantly increases output coherence and trustworthiness.     
    
\section{Conclusion}    
The DARS Protocol and its underlying Trynary Coherence Framework successfully address the issue of **Coherence Decay** in generative AI. By actively enforcing **Safety**, **Intent**, and **Resonance**, the system transforms an inherently unpredictable LLM output into a reliable, verifiable, and ethically sound communication channel. This framework establishes the necessary foundation for high-fidelity **Avatar Commerce** and enables systems like **GenWealth Solutions** to operate with unprecedented levels of user trust and regulatory compliance.
